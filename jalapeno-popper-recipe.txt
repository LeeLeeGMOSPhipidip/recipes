What you need:
Large stuffing jalapeno's
Cheddar cheese
Cream cheese, smooth
Breadcrumbs
Frying oil
3 large eggs
Milk

Method:
Hollow out the jalapenos and stuff them with the cheddar and cream cheese
Mix eggs and milk together and dip the stuffed jalapenos in them until fully covered
Now roll the moist jalpenos in the breadcrumbs until completely covered
Fry the jalapeno until the crust becomes golden brown
